With over 35 years of coaching experience across Westchester, Putnam, and Dutchess counties, Coach Bob Martin brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role as Director of Coaching at BYSC. Currently serving as the North Salem Varsity Girls Soccer Head Coach since 2005 and a dedicated coach at BYSC for over 30 years, Coach Martin has demonstrated expertise at all levels of sport, from collegiate to youth development.

His extensive career includes coaching Premier teams, League ODP, State ODP, and various travel programs for both boys and girls. Coach Martin holds a USSF National License, NYSPHSAA Coaching License, and New York State Teaching credentials, reflecting his commitment to education and athletic excellence.
