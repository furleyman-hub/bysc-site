Christiaan Cacciotti is a veteran coach with 34 years of experience building championship-caliber programs from the ground up. With 27 years at Bedford Youth Soccer and 18 years as Head of Training at Chappaqua AYSO, Christiaan has developed a keen eye for identifying player pathways and maximizing individual potential.

He built the Falcons and Hawks 2014 girls teams from age 5, advocating for them to compete in the EDP program, and led the Vipers Boys 2012 team to an impressive 19-game unbeaten run. Christiaan's coaching philosophy prioritizes technical, attractive soccer over winning at all costs, taking a long-term view toward player maturity and higher-level performance.
