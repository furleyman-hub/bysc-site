Ellie brings youthful energy and relatable coaching to Bedford Youth Soccer Club's youngest players. At 22, with over 18 years of playing experience including premier, tournament, varsity, and collegiate soccer, Ellie offers a fresh perspective as someone still actively playing at a competitive level. Holding a U.S. Soccer 11v11 Grassroots License, she coaches the 2016–2018 girls with natural connection and enthusiasm.

Ellie's coaching philosophy prioritizes development, joy, and connection — emphasizing ball mastery, dribbling, passing under pressure, and proper technique as the foundation for great players. Within a single season, she has helped players build confidence and technical skills through individualized attention, purposeful drills, and mini-games.
