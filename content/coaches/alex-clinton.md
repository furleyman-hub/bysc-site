Alex Clinton brings passion and premier-level playing experience to Bedford Youth Soccer Club. Holding both USSF Grassroots and D coaching licenses, Alex played for elite programs including F.C. Westchester, Everton F.C., and Beachside F.C. throughout his youth development. As 2018 Senior Captain of Fox Lane High School Varsity Soccer, he understands what it takes to lead on and off the field.

Now in his third season coaching the Bedford Tigers, Alex emphasizes U.S. Soccer's Play-Practice-Play framework and a positive culture built on unselfishness, kindness, respect, and integrity — helping the Tigers improve to a mid-table finish in their most recent season.
