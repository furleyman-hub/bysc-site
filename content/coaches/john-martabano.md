John Martabano is a dynamic coach whose energy and relatability make him a standout mentor for youth players. With over a decade of soccer involvement — from volunteer coaching at North Salem Elementary to Head Camp Counselor at Hendrie Soccer Academy — John has honed his ability to develop players at every level.

Currently Head Coach for Bedford Youth Soccer Club's 2013 and 2015 Boys teams, John has an impressive track record: he helped guide North Salem to a New York State Championship and transformed a Bedford 2013 team from a 1-win season to losing only 2 games the following year. His coaching philosophy prioritizes confidence-building, teamwork, and tactical awareness — because for John, coaching is about making a lasting impact on young lives, not just developing soccer players.
