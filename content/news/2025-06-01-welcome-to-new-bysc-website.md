We are thrilled to announce the launch of the brand-new Bedford Youth Soccer Club website!

This new site is designed to make it easier than ever to stay connected with the club. Here's what you'll find:

- **Programs & Teams** — Detailed information on every age group from U6 to U19
- **Schedule** — Up-to-date game and practice schedules
- **News & Announcements** — Club updates delivered right here
- **Registration** — Everything you need to sign your child up for the upcoming season

We'll be posting regularly with news about tryouts, season kick-offs, results, and events. Make sure to bookmark the site and check back often.

As always, you can reach us at [info@bysc.org](mailto:info@bysc.org) with any questions.

See you on the pitch! ⚽
