Registration for the **Fall 2025 season** is now open for all recreational programs!

## Who Can Register

- **U6 Tiny Tots** — Ages 4–6
- **U8 Mini Kickers** — Ages 7–8
- **U10 Rising Stars** — Ages 9–10
- **U12 Juniors** — Ages 11–12

## How to Register

Visit our [Registration page](/registration/) for full instructions, fees, and the registration link. Registration is first-come, first-served — we encourage families to register as soon as possible to secure a spot.

## Season Dates

- **First Practice:** Week of September 8, 2025
- **Games Begin:** September 20, 2025
- **Season Ends:** November 1, 2025

## Questions?

Contact us at [info@bysc.org](mailto:info@bysc.org) or speak with your child's coach from last season. We look forward to a great fall!
