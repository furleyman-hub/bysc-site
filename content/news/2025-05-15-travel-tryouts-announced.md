BYSC is pleased to announce **travel team tryouts** for the upcoming Fall 2025 season. We are forming teams at the **U12, U14, U16, and U19** age groups.

## Tryout Schedule

| Age Group | Date | Time | Location |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| U12       | June 7, 2025 | 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM | Bedford Town Fields |
| U14       | June 8, 2025 | 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM | Bedford Town Fields |
| U16 / U19 | June 8, 2025 | 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM   | Bedford Town Fields |

## What to Bring

- Cleats and shin guards (required)
- Water bottle
- Comfortable athletic clothing
- A positive attitude!

## Who Should Try Out

Any player aged 11–19 who is looking for a more competitive soccer experience is welcome. Prior travel team experience is helpful but **not required**. Our coaches evaluate players on athleticism, attitude, and coachability.

## Registration for Tryouts

Please [email us](mailto:info@bysc.org) to register your child for tryouts so we can plan for group sizes. Walk-ins are welcome but pre-registration is appreciated.

We look forward to seeing everyone on the field!
