## Season Registration

BYSC runs two main seasons each year: **Spring** (March–May) and **Fall** (September–November). Registration opens several weeks before each season begins. Check the [News page](/news/) for announcements.

## How to Register

1. **Read the program descriptions** on the [Programs & Teams](/programs/) page to find the right fit for your child.
2. **Click the registration link** above (or below) when registration is open.
3. **Complete the online form** — you'll need your child's birth date, emergency contact, and payment info.
4. **Watch for an email confirmation** from BYSC with your team assignment and first practice date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="faq-list">
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What age group does my child belong in?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Age groups are determined by your child's age as of August 1 of the current year. A child who turns 8 before August 1 plays U8; a child who turns 9 before August 1 plays U10, and so on. If you're unsure, email us and we'll help.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">How much does it cost?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Recreational fees vary by age group and season, typically ranging from $75–$150. Travel program fees are higher due to league registration and uniforms. Exact fees are listed on the registration form when open. Financial assistance is available — contact us at info@bysc.org.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What equipment does my child need?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      All players need shin guards and soccer cleats (or sneakers for U6). A size-appropriate soccer ball is recommended for practice at home. Travel team players receive a club uniform. Recreational players wear any soccer attire.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">Can I request a specific team or coach?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Team assignments for recreational players are made by club coordinators based on age, geography, and team balance. We cannot guarantee specific team assignments, but we do our best to accommodate sibling requests when possible.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What is your refund policy?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Full refunds are available if requested before the season start date. After the season begins, a partial refund may be issued at the club's discretion for medical reasons. Contact info@bysc.org for refund requests.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">My child has never played before — is that okay?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Absolutely! Our recreational programs welcome players of all experience levels, including complete beginners. Our coaches are experienced in working with first-time players and will make sure your child feels welcome and supported.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
