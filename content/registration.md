## Fall 2026 In-House Registration

Registration for the **Fall 2026 In-House season** is now open. Season dates will be announced shortly — register early to secure your spot.

**Travel program registration for 2026–27 is closed.** Watch the [News page](/news/) for future travel tryout announcements.

## How to Register

1. **Read the program descriptions** on the [Programs & Teams](/programs/) page to find the right fit for your child.
2. **Click the registration link** above when registration is open.
3. **Complete the online form** — you'll need your child's birth date, emergency contact, and payment info.
4. **Watch for an email confirmation** from BYSC with your team assignment and first practice date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="faq-list">
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What age group does my child belong in?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Age groups are based on your child's birth year per US Youth Soccer guidelines. If you're unsure which group your child belongs in, email us and we'll help.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">How much does it cost?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Recreational fees vary by age group. Exact fees are listed on the registration form. Financial assistance may be available — contact us at info@bysc.org.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What equipment does my child need?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      All players need shin guards and soccer cleats (sneakers are fine for the youngest age groups). A size-appropriate soccer ball is recommended for home practice. Travel team players receive a club uniform kit. In-house players wear any soccer attire.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">What is the difference between In-House and Travel?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      In-House is our recreational program — fun, community-focused, and coached by parent volunteers with professional trainers. Travel (WYSL) is our competitive program for more dedicated players who try out and compete against other clubs in Westchester.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">Can I request a specific team or coach?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Team assignments for in-house players are made by club coordinators based on age and team balance. We cannot guarantee specific assignments but do our best to accommodate sibling requests when possible.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <div class="faq-q">My child has never played before — is that okay?</div>
    <div class="faq-a">
      Absolutely! Our in-house program welcomes players of all experience levels. Our coaches are experienced working with first-time players and will make sure your child feels welcome.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
