## Fall 2026 In-House Registration

Registration for the **Fall 2026 In-House season** is now open. Season dates will be announced shortly — register early to secure your spot.

**Travel program registration for 2026–27 is closed.** Watch the [News page](/news/) for future travel tryout announcements.

## How to Register

1. **Read the program descriptions** on the [Programs & Teams](/programs/) page to find the right fit for your child.
2. **Click the registration link** above when registration is open.
3. **Complete the online form** — you'll need your child's birth date, emergency contact, and payment info.
4. **Watch for an email confirmation** from BYSC with your team assignment and first session date.

## Fees & Discounts

Registration dates are communicated via email and posted on this website. Exact fees are listed on the registration form when open.

**Family discount:** Families with **three or more children** enrolled in the In-House program receive a discount. Contact [bedfordsoccer1@gmail.com](mailto:bedfordsoccer1@gmail.com) for details.

*Uniforms (red BYSC shirt) and shin guards are required and purchased separately. Cleats are recommended but not required.*

## Scholarships

**Player Scholarships:** Financial assistance is available for families with financial hardships. Contact the Registrar at [mfenn@ix.netcom.com](mailto:mfenn@ix.netcom.com) to learn more about qualifications or to submit a scholarship request.

**High School Student Scholarships:** BYSC also offers scholarships to local high school students who actively participate in the league and make a significant contribution to the program. Contact the board President or Vice-President for more information.

## Refund Policy

| When | Refund |
|------|--------|
| Before the 3rd session of the season | 50% refund |
| After participating in the 3rd session or any session thereafter | No refund |
