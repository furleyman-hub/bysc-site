## About This Program

The U12 Juniors program offers a competitive recreational experience. Players build technical skills, understand team formations, and compete in local league games.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** 9v9 games, full recreational league schedule
- **Season:** Spring and Fall
- **Schedule:** Two practices per week + weekend games
- **Tryouts:** None required — open enrollment
- **Equipment:** Shin guards, cleats, size 4 soccer ball
