## About This Program

The U10 Rising Stars program introduces more structure and team strategy while keeping the focus on skill development and enjoyment. Players begin to understand roles and positions.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** 7v7 games
- **Season:** Spring and Fall
- **Schedule:** Weekly practice + Saturday games
- **Tryouts:** None required — open enrollment
- **Equipment:** Shin guards, cleats, size 4 soccer ball
