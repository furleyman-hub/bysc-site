## About This Program

Our U14–U19 travel program is BYSC's flagship competitive offering. Coaches at this level have extensive experience and many hold USSF coaching licenses. This program is for players who are serious about soccer and committed to improvement.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** 11v11, competitive regional and state leagues
- **Season:** Year-round (with scheduled breaks)
- **Schedule:** 3 practices per week + weekend games/tournaments
- **Tryouts:** Required — held annually in late spring
- **Travel:** Regional and state-wide; some out-of-state tournaments
- **Equipment:** Club-issued uniform; players supply cleats, shin guards, size 5 ball

## Player Development

Players at this level receive advanced coaching focused on:
- Technical skill refinement
- Tactical understanding (formations, pressing, transition play)
- Physical conditioning and injury prevention
- Mental preparation and game-day readiness

## College Recruiting Support

BYSC supports older players (U16–U19) in navigating the college soccer recruiting process, including guidance on highlight videos, showcases, and contacting college coaches.
