## In-House Program

BYSC's In-House program is the starting point for many young players and their first exposure to organized sports. We focus on keeping things simple, positive, and fun while developing basic skills — dribbling, passing, and shooting — alongside coordination and motor skills. Our goal is to provide the foundation players need to eventually transition to our Travel Program and beyond.

Players are placed according to **school grade**, not age. Sessions run on Saturday mornings and are led by professional coaches, approved volunteer parent coaches, and junior trainers.

### Equipment

All players need:
- Red BYSC In-House shirt
- Shorts and soccer socks
- **Shin guards** (required)
- Soccer cleats (recommended, not required)
- Water bottle

BYSC provides soccer balls. Equipment can be purchased at **Mount Kisco Sports**, 13 East Main Street, Mount Kisco, NY 10543.

### Weather & Cancellations

In the event of inclement weather or lightning, cancellations will be posted on this website and sent via email notification. Please check back on game day if conditions are uncertain.

### Parent Guidelines

- Parents are **not permitted on the field** during sessions, which are run by professional and volunteer coaches.
- Parents are **required to stay** for the entire duration of their child's session.
- We welcome parents who want to help — contact us if you're interested in volunteering as a coach or assistant.
- Please be careful in the parking lot, as we share the park with other sports and residents.
