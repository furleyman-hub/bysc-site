## About This Program

The U6 Tiny Tots program is designed for children ages 4–6 who are experiencing organized soccer for the first time. The focus is entirely on fun, movement, and building a love for the game.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** Small group activities and 3v3 mini-games
- **Season:** Spring and Fall
- **Schedule:** Saturday mornings (approximately 1 hour)
- **Tryouts:** None required — open enrollment
- **Equipment:** Shin guards, cleats or sneakers, size 3 soccer ball
