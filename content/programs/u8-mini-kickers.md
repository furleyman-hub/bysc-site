## About This Program

The U8 program builds on foundational skills learned at the U6 level. Players work on individual technique while beginning to understand simple team concepts like passing and positioning.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** 4v4 games on small fields
- **Season:** Spring and Fall
- **Schedule:** Saturday games + one weekday practice
- **Tryouts:** None required — open enrollment
- **Equipment:** Shin guards, cleats, size 3 soccer ball
