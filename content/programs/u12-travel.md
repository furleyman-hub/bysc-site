## About This Program

BYSC's U12 Travel program is for dedicated players ready to step up to a more competitive environment. Players train multiple times per week and compete against teams from surrounding towns and cities.

## What to Expect

- **Format:** 11v11, regional travel league
- **Season:** Year-round (Fall season + Spring season)
- **Schedule:** 2–3 practices per week + weekend games/tournaments
- **Tryouts:** Required — held in late spring
- **Travel:** Regional (eastern Massachusetts)
- **Equipment:** Club-issued uniform (included in fees); cleats, shin guards, size 5 ball

## Tryout Information

Tryout dates are announced each spring via the [News](/news/) page and email. All U10 and U12 recreational players in good standing are encouraged to try out.

## Cost

Travel program fees are higher than recreational due to league registration, tournament entry, and uniform costs. See the [Registration](/registration/) page for details.
