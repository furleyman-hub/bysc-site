## How Schedules Work

Individual team schedules are distributed by your child's coach at the start of each season. The calendar below shows all BYSC games, practices, and events.

**To add BYSC events to your own Google Calendar**, click the **+** icon in the bottom-right corner of the calendar below.

---

*The full schedule calendar will appear here once connected. To add your Google Calendar, visit the [Site Settings in the admin panel](/admin/) and paste your Google Calendar embed URL.*

---

## Schedule Reminders

- **Game cancellations** due to weather are announced via email and posted on the [News page](/news/).
- **Field locations** are included in your team's schedule distributed by the coach.
- Makeup games are scheduled at the discretion of the league coordinator.

## Contact

Questions about your team's schedule? Reach out to your coach directly, or contact the club at [info@bysc.org](mailto:info@bysc.org).
